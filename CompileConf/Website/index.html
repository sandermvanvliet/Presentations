Frequently Asked Questions

Where will the conference be held?

The Compile conference will be held at the Rabobank Europastaete (UEP) building in Utrecht.

I'm not based in Utrecht, how can I follow the conference?

We aim to provide live-streams of the Compile conference. More information should be available later.

I'm not a developer, can I attend Compile?

Yes! The Compile conference is targetted at developers but we welcome everybody with an interest in the software development process.

What will it cost me?

The good news is: You don't need to buy a ticket to go to Compile!

When it comes to your timesheet you can book 2 hours on your regular project code, the other 2 hours will either go on your training budget (for internals) or are your own investment (for externals).
If you've got any questions about this drop us an e-mail.
